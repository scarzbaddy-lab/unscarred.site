What is Ground Zero?

Ground Zero is where everything got built. Before age seven, your nervous system decided how to survive. It built shields to protect you. Then masks grew over the shields. Then the world gave you labels for what you became.

This is not regular relationship advice. This is not "just communicate better." This is for people whose whole system organized around early chaos, and now you are trying to live a normal life while your body thinks it is still in that house.

If you feel like you are too much, too broken, too complicated for normal healing content, you are in the right place.

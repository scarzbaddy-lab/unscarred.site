Membership & Services

Transparent clarity on what we offer and what we don't

What Is Unscarred?

Unscarred is pattern translation and educational support. We help you understand your nervous system patterns and how they developed. We are NOT a therapy practice, and we do not provide medical care, psychiatric diagnosis, or crisis intervention.

⚠️ Important: If you are in crisis, experiencing suicidal thoughts, or in danger, contact emergency services (911 in the US) or a licensed mental health professional immediately.

Personal Support Membership: What's Included

✓ Real-Time Text Support

Access to steady, nervous-system-informed support when you're activated, stuck, or confused about your patterns.

✓ Pattern Reflection

Someone who can name what's happening underneath your surface behavior - not therapy analysis, but real-time pattern translation.

✓ Nervous System Support

Support tailored to your specific nervous system state (fight/flight/freeze/fawn) - not one-size-fits-all advice.

✓ Co-Regulation When High Load

A steady presence when your load is high, your window is compressed, and you need someone who speaks your nervous system language.

What's NOT Included

✗ Therapy or Clinical Treatment

Unscarred is not a licensed therapy practice. We don't diagnose, treat, or cure mental illness. Pattern translation is complementary to therapy, not a replacement.

✗ Crisis Support

If you're in acute crisis (suicidal ideation, psychosis, severe dissociation), you need emergency services. Membership is for pattern understanding, not crisis stabilization.

✗ Medical or Psychiatric Advice

We don't prescribe medication, manage side effects, or replace psychiatry. If you need medication evaluation, see a psychiatrist.

✗ Diagnosis

We don't diagnose ADHD, autism, BPD, CPTSD, or any condition. We help you understand patterns. For diagnosis, see a licensed clinician.

✗ Guarantee of Instant Change

Pattern understanding doesn't instantly fix patterns. Real change is slow, nervous-system-dependent, and requires repeated experience of safety.

Boundaries & Response Time

Response Windows

Text support is available during business hours with reasonably prompt responses. This is not 24/7 crisis hotline - it's steady support within realistic time frames.

What We Can't Do in Real-Time

  • Provide emergency response (call 911 or crisis line instead)
  • Replace hospitalization or crisis psychiatric care
  • Provide medication management or medical advice
  • Work with active psychosis without concurrent psychiatric care
  • Replace mandatory reporting situations (abuse, safety concerns go to authorities)
Mandatory Reporting: If you disclose intent to harm yourself or others, we are mandated to report. We will not keep this confidential.

Ideal Fit For Membership

You might benefit from Unscarred if:

  • You have a therapist but want nervous-system-first pattern naming
  • You understand your trauma but can't seem to change the patterns
  • You feel stuck between "I understand why" and "I can actually change"
  • You need someone who speaks nervous system language, not diagnostic language
  • You're actively doing personal work and want a pattern mirror

This might not be the fit if:

  • You need crisis stabilization (needs psychiatry/emergency services)
  • You're untreated for active psychosis (needs psychiatric care first)
  • You need medication management (needs a psychiatrist)
  • You want to avoid clinical care (you probably need it)
  • You want instant change without nervous system work (it doesn't work that way)

Confidentiality & Privacy

Your patterns and conversations are private within legal limits. The exceptions are mandatory reporting situations (harm to self/others, abuse, neglect).

This is not HIPAA-protected like therapy. It's educational support with privacy expectations but legal reporting obligations.

Questions?
